About the Club

Shaping the Economic Leaders of Tomorrow

EconCore is a dynamic community of students, researchers, and policy enthusiasts committed to understanding and shaping the economic forces that drive our world. We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application.

Our Mission

To cultivate economic literacy and research excellence among students through hands-on projects, debate, and collaboration.

Our Vision

A world where every student has the tools to analyze, question, and improve the economic systems shaping society.

Achievements

Published {{ blogs|length + podcasts|length }} research papers/blogs, hosted 30+ guest lectures, won 3 intercollegiate economics competitions.

Global Reach

Members from 12+ academic disciplines collaborating on locally relevant and globally impactful economic research.

Why Economics Matters

  • Understand market dynamics
  • Shape public policy
  • Drive innovation & growth
  • Solve inequality & poverty
  • Predict global trends
  • Empower informed decisions
